about

Hello! I'm Saif and i'm a Web Developer.

I was born and bought up in a place called Bhilai which is situated in Chhattisgarh, India. My fascination for computers began since i was 4 years old and when my dad got a pentium 2 processor pc. I used to explore all the things that it can do, run everything i could find. Eventually, I started to understand that this is something i can be really good at. Skipping a few years, i completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2018.

After that, I started my career in the field of web development, in which i learned about new technologies and how to make use of them to fulfil my tasks and projects frequently. I currently work on Frontend part of the web, and my main programming languages are HTML, CSS and Javascript. Alongside i got familiar with various libraries and frameworks that helped me build more efficient and visually appealing web apps, websites. I got an overview of how the web works, both frontend and backend part and how to make use of third party applications for my purpose.

Why choose this field at all you ask? Well, to put it in a simplified way, i enjoy creating new things and later feel proud of what i've achieved. 😀

I also have a few other hobbies and interests like gaming, music production, watching movies/tv-series.
